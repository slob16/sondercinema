Sonder Cinema is a dynamic production company based in the heart of Utah, founded by two passionate filmmakers. With a keen eye for detail and a love for storytelling, they specialize in creating films that resonate with authenticity and emotion. Drawing inspiration from both the landscapes of Utah and the diverse experiences of everyday life, Sonder Cinema brings stories to life that are as visually striking as they are deeply personal.

About our Film Rawlins Rawlins is the first feature film by Sonder Cinema Group, produced by Cameron Babcock, written & directed by James Hall & executive produced by Sian Breward.

To rescue her father from self-destruction, Jayden Rawlins, a small town girl, challenges fate by joining an all-boys high school wrestling team, facing relentless opposition from the town, the team, and the coach determined to break her spirit.
